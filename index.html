Company Profile

About Company

Hacktiv8 is a leading digital education platform in Indonesia that focuses on developing digital and technology skills through intensive bootcamp programs. In partnership with IBM, we provide cutting-edge curriculum that combines industry expertise with IBM's technological leadership.

Our programs at Hacktiv8 are designed with a project-based approach, supported by professional mentors from leading companies in Southeast Asia, and a curriculum that is relevant to industry needs with IBM's certification programs.

Additionally, Hacktiv8 also offers career support through guidance, interview preparation, and internship opportunities, as well as payment flexibility through "Study Now, Pay Later" options to facilitate access for participants.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be the leading higher education platform in Southeast Asia that provides access to high-quality education relevant to industry needs, in partnership with IBM, to empower individuals to achieve their maximum professional potential.

Mission:

  • Provide high-quality education that meets industry needs with IBM technology integration
  • Help students transform their careers through practical training
  • Empower the Hacktiv8 team to grow together in the company
  • Create beneficial opportunities for every individual involved
